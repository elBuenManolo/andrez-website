Freshly Made in Borgartún

At Andrez, we believe in the simple joy of a well-prepared meal. Located in the bustling heart of Borgartún, our neighborhood kitchen is dedicated to serving up generous, comforting dishes that make you feel right at home.

Whether you're craving one of our signature sweet or savory crepes, digging into our authentic French Tacos — a unique toasted delight filled with meat, fries, and our signature cheesy sauce, distinctly different from Mexican tacos — or just looking for a friendly spot to grab lunch, we're here to deliver quality food with a warm smile.
